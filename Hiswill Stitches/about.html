Welcome to Hiswill Stitches

Crafting Elegance, One Stitch at a Time At hiswill stitches, we believe that every garment tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Lagos City, our boutique tailor shop has been a haven for discerning individuals seeking sartorial excellence since 2015.


Our Story

Founded by Godswill, our journey into the world of tailoring began with a simple passion for fabric and fashion. From humble beginnings as an apprentice, I honed my skills under the guidance of master tailors, learning the artistry and precision required to create garments of unparalleled quality.

Driven by a desire to blend tradition with innovation, I established Hiswill Stitches to offer a bespoke experience that celebrates individuality and craftsmanship. Each garment that leaves our atelier is a testament to our dedication to timeless style and impeccable fit.


Our Services

At Hiswill Stitches, we specialize in crafting Senators wears, dresses, and alterations tailored to your unique preferences. Whether it's a bespoke wear for a special occasion or a delicate alteration to restore the perfect fit, our team of skilled artisans is committed to exceeding your expectations.
